# Privacy Policy for Markdown Web Clipper

**Effective Date:** March 19, 2026

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

"Markdown Web Clipper" (hereinafter referred to as "the Extension") respects your privacy. The Extension does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data, web browsing history, or the content you clip.

## 2. Local Processing

All functions of the Extension, including extracting main content and converting it to Markdown format, are performed entirely locally on your device (within your browser). No data is ever sent to any external servers or third parties.

## 3. Permissions

The Extension requires the following permissions solely for its core functionality:

- `activeTab`: To access the current tab only when you explicitly click the extension icon or use a shortcut.
- `scripting`: To inject the script needed to extract and convert the page content into Markdown.
- `clipboardWrite`: To copy the generated Markdown text directly to your clipboard.
- `storage`: To save user preferences for the extension's behavior (e.g., whether to include images in the Markdown output). This data is stored locally and is not shared or transmitted.
